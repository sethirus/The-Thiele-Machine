## Clarification: What "Graph Reconstruction Bridge" Means

**The hardware performs all 47 operations in silicon.** `kami_step` in
`Abstraction.v` directly updates partition table entries, rich-state morph
tables, and tensor registers for every opcode. Nothing is delegated to
software at runtime.

`GraphReconstructionBridge.v` (formerly `DriverManaged.v`) is a **proof bridge
file**, not a runtime architecture. Its theorems say: "the hardware tables
updated by `kami_step`, when reconstructed by `snap_pt_to_graph` and
`snapshot_morphisms_of_rich_state`, produce the same abstract `VMState` as
`vm_apply`."
